Chamomile Ice Cream

Description

This recipe is great for when you want the cozy comfort of a sweet chamomile tea, but it's too hot for hot drinks.

Ingredients

  • 2c whole milk
  • 2.5c heavy whipping cream
  • 1c sugar (better yet, use honey)
  • 6 - 8 chamomile tea bags

Steps

  1. Bring whole milk to a light simmer in a sauce pan.
  2. Add the sugar (or honey) and chamomile tea bags, cover, and let steep over night while the milk cools.
  3. Remove the tea bags and add heavy whipping cream. Blend just enough so it is evenly dispersed.
  4. Follow ice cream maker's instructions to turn the delicious liquid into ice cream consistency.
