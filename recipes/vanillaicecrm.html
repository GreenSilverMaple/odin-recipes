Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

Just like how a real amazing cheese pizza signals a master pizza maker, a well done vanilla ice cream speaks highly of your ice cream making skills.

Ingredients

  • 2c whole milk
  • 2.5c heavy whipping cream
  • 1c sugar
  • 2tbsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Thoroughly mix whole milk and sugar.
  2. Add vanilla extract and heavy whipping cream, and blend just enough to evenly disperse the ingredients.
  3. Follow ice cream maker's instructions to turn the delicious liquid into ice cream consistency.
